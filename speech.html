In B-20's theme - RAISE, ‘I’ represents
Innovation. But along with innovation, I also
see another ‘I’ in it - Inclusiveness: PM Modi

PM Modi addressed the B20 Summit India 2023 in New Delhi. Speaking about the B20 theme ‘R.A.I.S.E.’, the Prime Minister said that even though the ‘I’ represents innovation but he pictures another ‘I’ of inclusiveness. Talking about the lessons learnt from the once in a century calamity, the Covid-19 pandemic, PM Modi said that that the pandemic taught us that the thing that needs most of our investment is ‘mutual trust’.

Mission Chandrayaan has become a symbol
of the spirit of New India: PM Modi during
Mann Ki Baat

During Mann Ki Baat, Prime Minister Narendra Modi applauded ISRO scientists for the remarkable success of the Chandrayaan-3 lunar mission. He said, "Mission Chandrayaan has become a symbol of the spirit of New India." The PM spoke about various other subjects such as the upcoming G-20 Leaders' Summit, the praiseworthy performances of our sportspersons, the importance of mother tongue, Sanskrit language, India's vibrant dairy sector and more!
